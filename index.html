Meet the team

We are a network of designers, technologists, and researchers.

Interaction Designer

Andy is an interaction designer who creates human-centered technologies in areas such as health and wellbeing, education, and the creative sector. He specialises in the design and development of mobile and web technologies.

Software Engineer

Gerard is a software engineer who has worked for the past 4 years with the BBC on broadcast applications of sensing. He specialises in motion sensing technolgies with IMUs, and gestural control systems.
